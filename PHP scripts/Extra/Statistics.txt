At least 80% of humanity lives on less than $10 a day.\n
More than 80 percent of the world population lives in countries where income differentials are widening.\n
Nearly a billion people entered the 21st century unable to read a book or sign their names.\n
According to UNICEF, 22,000 children die each day due to poverty.They die quietly in some of the poorest villages on earth, far removed from the scrutiny and the conscience of the world. Being meek and weak in life makes these dying multitudes even more invisible in death.\n
